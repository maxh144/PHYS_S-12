About Me


Hello, I'm Max Hughes. I am 16 and a rising junior. I live and go to highschool in Cohasset, Massachusets, a small town on the ocean about one hour south of Boston. I have been building robots for 8 years, focusing on 3D printed contraptions and Arduinos. I have the most experience in programming microcontrollers and CAD. I have built numerous robots and vehicles, most of which have not worked well, learing a lot along the way. Outside of school and digital fabrication, I enjoy skiing, playing video games, random trivia, and learning about the world. I'm very talkative and try to always stay positive.
